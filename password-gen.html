Guidelines for good passwords
  • Don't reuse passwords across multiple accounts, especially for social, banking and email.
  • Consider a Password Manager (such as Bitwarder or Lastpass)
  • Use at least 10 characters; The longer, the better.
  • Try combining 2-4 random words and a 4-6 digit number you can easily memorize.
  • Avoid personal details like ID, phone, name, birthdate etc (yours or your family's).
  • Avoid misspelling or adding number/symbol at random places - it can be confusing and have little effect on password security.
  • Beware of phishing - be suspicious if you need to enter a password after clicking a link on your phone or email.
